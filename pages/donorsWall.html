University Donation & Contribution Fund

University Donation & Contribution Fund is a program that allows individuals and organizations to donate money to universities to support academic programs, research, scholarships, and other important initiatives. These contributions help DTU to provide an excellent education and make a positive impact on society.

University Corpus Fund

The University Corpus Fund is a corpus fund that is created by the University to support the University’s academic and research activities. The corpus fund is created by the University by investing the donations received from the alumni, industry, and other sources. The corpus fund is invested in the market and the returns from the corpus fund are used to support the University’s academic and research activities.

DTU Medals & Scholarships

DTU Medals and Scholarships are academic honors and financial awards that recognize outstanding achievements of students in various fields of study. DTU awards these medals and scholarships to support and encourage academic excellence, research, innovation, and leadership skills among its students. The university offers a range of scholarships and medals, including merit-based scholarships, PhD scholarships, innovation scholarships, and more, to support deserving students in their academic pursuits.
